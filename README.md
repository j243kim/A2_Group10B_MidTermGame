# TBI Game: Collect the Stars

## Group Number

Group 10B

## Description

A p5.js game designed to foster understanding of Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI) through engaging gameplay mechanics. Players experience challenges that reflect aspects of living with TBI, promoting empathy and awareness without relying on stereotypes or pity.

The game focuses on translating challenges related to TBI into gameplay systems rather than explaining them only through text. Players interact with mechanics that simulate difficulties with memory and sensory overload.

One core mechanic is **Memory Fade**. At the beginning of the level, the objective is shown to the player, but after a short period the text disappears. Players can press **M** to briefly recall the objective. This mechanic represents how people with TBI may sometimes struggle to maintain task instructions or remember goals during an activity.

Another mechanic is **Sensory Overload**. An overload meter gradually increases while the player moves through the level. If the overload reaches the maximum level, the player loses. A **Calm Zone** in the environment allows players to reduce the overload level. This reflects the experience of overstimulation and the need to manage sensory input.

Together, these mechanics create a gameplay loop where players must manage attention, memory, and environmental awareness while completing the level objective.

The game also includes a **Low Sensory Mode** (press L) that reduces visual effects like pulsing for players who are sensitive to stimulation.

## Setup and Interaction Instructions

1. Open the game via the GitHub Pages link, or open `index.html` in Google Chrome.
2. Press **ENTER** to start the game.
3. Use the **Arrow Keys** to move the player.
4. Press **M** to recall the objective if it fades.
5. Move into the **Calm Zone** to reduce the overload meter.
6. Collect all stars in the level to complete the objective.
7. If the overload meter reaches its maximum level, the game ends.
8. Press L at anytime to toggle Low Sensory Mode to reduct visual effects.
